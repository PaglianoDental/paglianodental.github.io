About me

Dr. Matt Pagliano is a compassionate and dedicated dentist with a strong focus on aesthetics and oral hygiene. He is passionate about helping you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile, and believes that everyone, regardless of their dental history, can reach this goal. Dr. Pagliano creates a welcoming environment where no one needs to feel self-conscious about their teeth—he’s excited to help you feel confident in your smile. With a commitment to thorough care, he ensures you receive all the information necessary to maintain your perfect teeth.

Dr. Pagliano offers a wide range of advanced treatments tailored to meet your individual needs, including dental implants, Invisalign, and full mouth rehabilitation. Whether you're looking to restore missing teeth with implants, straighten your smile discreetly with Invisalign, or undergo a complete restoration to improve both function and aesthetics, Dr. Pagliano uses the latest techniques and technology to ensure optimal results. He takes the time to thoroughly discuss your options, creating personalized treatment plans that prioritize both your dental health and your long-term smile goals.
